Business: portfolio
Keywords: personal, portfolio, dark
Main Font: Montserrat
Body Font: Roboto
Font Justification: Montserrat is a geometric sans-serif font that conveys confidence, modernity, and structure—perfect for titles and 
headers in a professional portfolio. Its bold and clean lines establish a strong visual identity. Roboto, a versatile and highly legible 
sans-serif, complements it by offering clarity and balance for longer texts. Together, they create a contemporary aesthetic that feels 
both approachable and refined.
Primary Color: #0A0A0A
Secondary Color: #FFFFFF
Accent Color: #1C1C1C
Palette Justification: The primary color, a deep charcoal gray (#0A0A0A), sets a bold and sophisticated foundation that enhances the 
visual weight of the portfolio. The secondary tone (#FFFFFF) introduces brightness and contrast, ensuring readability and balance within 
the dark theme. The accent shade (#1C1C1C) adds subtle depth and texture, unifying the palette and evoking a timeless, minimalist 
aesthetic that aligns with the brand’s refined and modern personality.
